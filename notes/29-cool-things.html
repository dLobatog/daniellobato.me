29 things that make you a cool person

  1. People who warn others about potential dangers (Broken glass on the floor)
  2. People who include others within their group
  3. People who wait while you tie your shoes
  4. People holding the door for someone (from a close distance)
  5. When someone texts to get home safe and if you forget, they ask if you got home
  6. When they listen and not make the conversation about them
  7. That one friend who wishes you happy birthday at midnight
  8. When someone hypes up your taste in music
  9. When someone notices a change in your appearance
  10. When someone asks if you want anything from the store
  11. People who get excited when you get excited
  12. The person who doesn't let you stand awkwardly when you run into someone they know. Instead, they introduce you to them.
  13. When there are no seats and someone scooches over so you have enough space
  14. People who actually say nice things when they think about you
  15. When you're telling a story/joke and one person pays attention while everyone else ignores you
  16. When you have a couple of grocery items and a person with a full cart lets you go ahead of them
  17. When someone picks up a grocery item that fell rather than walk past it
  18. When someone lets someone else in when there's traffic
  19. People saying good morning and goodbye (especially at work)
  20. Offering help or asking if they're okay when you see someone struggling
  21. When someone brings snacks/food
  22. People who remember small details that you brought up casually in conversation
  23. When someone smiles at your dog
  24. When someone says "I'd like for you to join" instead of "You can join, if you want"
  25. When someone actually responds to the thing you recommended them ("Hey, I listened to that song you recommended to me")
  26. People who are patient with you
  27. People who hype you up when you struggle until you finally get it right
  28. People who thank people offering a service (Uber drivers, waiters, etc.)
  29. Give a smile. Say hi, please, and thank you
